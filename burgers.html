ABOUT US


MOS Burgers is a restaurant that contains with Fried and bakery goods. On 09th March 1997 opened the first restaurant for our customers in Rathmalana. On 06th November 2003 we opened our new food store in Colombo for easy way to transport our products. At about 2006 we have came to one of the top names of restaurant brands in this country. Then we opened our Second restaurant in Colombo on 20 th July 2006. Our products have sold in Colombo area and customers have came from other provinces to buy our products. Now we have more than 25 restaurants around this country. And also tourists also coming into our restaurants and they said our products have a delicious sweet and spicy flavour. We hope your support for our restaurants. We think you must like and love to eat our food.

We make our products by using fresh ingredients. They haven't any harmful things in them. We bake and cook our food for a healthy life. We didn't use oil too much to fry our bakery goods. Some people tell it is good way to their health. We think you must leave a like to our restaurant and food. Thank you so much!
